Things to do

Eat at the Diner

Indulge in the local charm at our classic diner, where the aroma of home-cooked meals welcomes you. From sizzling burgers to mouthwatering pies, every bite at the diner is a taste of our town's heritage.

An evening at the Bogs

Experience tranquility amidst nature by enjoying an evening swim in our serene bogs. As the sun sets, the water mirrors the colorful sky, creating a magical atmosphere perfect for unwinding after a bustling day.

See our historic Church

Explore the rich history of our town by visiting the iconic historic church. Meet Father McCaughtry, a pillar of our community, whose warmth and knowledge breathe life into the stories embedded within these ancient walls.

Grab a drink at the bar

Quench your thirst and mingle with friendly faces at our beloved town bar. Sip on local brews and unwind in the cozy ambiance, where laughter and shared stories echo through the night, making every drink a memory to cherish.

Where to stay

Everyone in our community picks one out of our two living arrangements. Be sure to choose wisely.

Colony House

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the enchanting heritage of our town by choosing the Colony House. Nestled amidst historic cobblestone streets and rustic architecture, this charming abode echoes tales of the past. With its quaint design and timeless ambiance, the Colony House offers a unique living experience, inviting you to become part of the town's living history. Embrace the sense of community within these storied walls, where each nook whispers a story and every corner evokes a nostalgic charm that's hard to resist.

OR

The Township

Experience modern comfort while being seamlessly woven into the vibrant fabric of our town by choosing a house within the township. Enjoy the convenience of contemporary amenities and the freedom to infuse your own style into your living space. Your township house provides easy access to local attractions, bustling markets, and a dynamic social scene, ensuring a lifestyle that's both connected and personalized. Revel in the fusion of modernity and community spirit as you create your haven within the heart of our thriving town.
